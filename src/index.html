about

Hi, I'm Aniebiet Afia, an aspiring software engineer based in Abuja, Nigeria. I'm passionate about building software that solves real-world problems and improves the lives of those who use it. I aspire to become a world-class Backend engineer writing reusable, testable and efficient codes and building scalable products. I'm looking for opportunities to work with a team of talented engineers to build amazing software.
